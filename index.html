    • Abstract

    Hi there, I'm Baptiste, 25 years old, living in Grenoble, France. I currently work as a software engineer for a French company called Arturia where I develop music related software.

    I'm (mostly) interested in music production, interaction design and software development, best case scenario being a combination of the three fields.

    What i do

    Development

    I mainly develop cross-platform audio plugins using C++, the VST3 SDK and JUCE. I like to play with the latest experimental libraries that I can find on GitHub. And because I like challenges, audio related development on the Android platform is something I really enjoy.

    Music Production

    Using Ableton Live, my Elektron drum machine and my modular synth, I try to assemble a variety of noises to create some industrial techno vibe. I'm also a lot into lofi / break / experimental stuff. When I have the occasion, I play other people tracks under the names of Dislocated or Make Install.

    My personal projects

    Matrix

    Simple drum sequencer for the Novation Launchpad powered by the Web Audio / Midi API.

    Slider

    Remote MIDI control Android app, bluetooth powered.
